        Makeup Day Finder

        Find makeup slots by joining existing sessions only. Simulation makeup joins weeks where another student already has that Sim (1–17). Clinical makeup prefers joining an existing clinical at the student's oriented facility; Week 18 is last resort for both. Sim-on-clinical-week conflicts prioritize simulation — the clinical is marked missed, then search for clinical makeup (facility join first). Overload slots are makeup-only when a session is at normal capacity.
